Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How do I book a tour package?

You can book a tour package through our website by selecting your desired package and clicking on the "Book Now" button. Alternatively, you can contact our team via phone, email, or by visiting our office. We'll guide you through the booking process and help you customize your itinerary if needed.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, and bank transfers. For international clients, we also accept PayPal. A 25% deposit is required to confirm your booking, with the remaining balance due 30 days before your trip starts.

Can I customize a tour package?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating customized travel experiences. You can either modify one of our existing packages or work with our travel experts to create a completely personalized itinerary based on your preferences, budget, and time constraints.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy varies depending on the package and timing of cancellation. Generally, cancellations made 45 days or more before departure receive a full refund minus a small administrative fee. Cancellations between 30-45 days receive a 75% refund, 15-30 days receive a 50% refund, and less than 15 days may not be eligible for a refund. Please refer to the specific terms and conditions for your package.

Do you provide assistance with visa applications?

Yes, we provide guidance and assistance with visa applications for international travelers visiting India. While we don't directly process visas, we can provide the necessary documentation to support your application and advise you on the requirements for your specific nationality.
