Our Story

Founded in 2010, Wanderlust Travels began with a simple vision: to help travelers discover the incredible beauty and diversity of India. What started as a small operation with just three passionate travel enthusiasts has now grown into one of the most trusted travel agencies in the country.

Our journey has been filled with countless adventures, challenges, and unforgettable experiences. Over the years, we've helped thousands of travelers from around the world explore the rich cultural heritage, breathtaking landscapes, and vibrant traditions that make India such a unique destination.

This philosophy guides everything we do, from designing our tour packages to training our guides and selecting our accommodation partners.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional travel experiences that showcase the authentic beauty of India while promoting sustainable tourism practices that benefit local communities and preserve our natural and cultural heritage.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted name in Indian travel, known for our personalized service, attention to detail, and commitment to creating journeys that transform and inspire our clients.
