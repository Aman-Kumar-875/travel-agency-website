Golden Triangle Tour

6 Days / 5 Nights

₹24,999 per person

Tour Overview

Experience the rich history and culture of North India with our Golden Triangle Tour. This classic itinerary takes you through Delhi, Agra, and Jaipur, showcasing the country's most iconic landmarks including the Taj Mahal, Red Fort, and Amber Palace.

Inclusions

  • 5 nights accommodation in 4-star hotels
  • Daily breakfast and dinner
  • Private air-conditioned vehicle for transportation
  • Professional English-speaking tour guide
  • All entrance fees to monuments
  • Welcome drink on arrival
  • All applicable taxes

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Delhi

Arrive at Delhi International Airport where our representative will meet you. Transfer to your hotel for check-in. Rest of the day at leisure. Overnight in Delhi.

Day 2: Delhi City Tour

After breakfast, enjoy a full-day sightseeing tour of Old and New Delhi. Visit Jama Masjid, Red Fort, Chandni Chowk, Raj Ghat, Humayun's Tomb, Qutub Minar, and India Gate. Overnight in Delhi.

Day 3: Delhi to Agra

After breakfast, drive to Agra (approx. 4 hours). Visit Agra Fort and witness the breathtaking Taj Mahal at sunset. Overnight in Agra.

Day 4: Agra to Jaipur via Fatehpur Sikri

Early morning visit to Taj Mahal at sunrise (optional). After breakfast, drive to Jaipur, visiting Fatehpur Sikri en route. Arrive in Jaipur by evening. Overnight in Jaipur.

Day 5: Jaipur City Tour

After breakfast, visit Amber Fort with an elephant ride to the entrance. Later, explore City Palace, Jantar Mantar Observatory, and Hawa Mahal. Enjoy shopping in the local markets. Overnight in Jaipur.

Day 6: Jaipur to Delhi - Departure

After breakfast, drive back to Delhi (approx. 5 hours). Transfer to the airport for your onward journey.
