Exploring the Serene Backwaters of Kerala

Kerala, often referred to as "God's Own Country," is famous for its lush landscapes, vibrant culture, and most notably, its tranquil backwaters. These interconnected canals, rivers, lakes, and lagoons run parallel to the Arabian Sea coast, creating a unique ecosystem that's both breathtaking and serene.
