Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is an ivory-white marble mausoleum on the right bank of the river Yamuna in Agra, Uttar Pradesh. It was commissioned in 1632 by the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan to house the tomb of his favorite wife, Mumtaz Mahal; it also houses the tomb of Shah Jahan himself.

The Taj Mahal was designated as a UNESCO World Heritage Site in 1983 for being "the jewel of Muslim art in India and one of the universally admired masterpieces of the world's heritage". It is regarded by many as the best example of Mughal architecture and a symbol of India's rich history.

Highlights

Stunning Architecture
Beautiful Gardens
UNESCO Heritage Site
Sunset Views

Best Time to Visit

October to March is the best time to visit the Taj Mahal when the weather is pleasant. Early morning visits offer fewer crowds and beautiful lighting for photography.

Pink City - Jaipur

Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, is known as the "Pink City" due to the distinctive color of its buildings. The city was founded in 1727 by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II, who was the ruler of Amber. The city's colorful, chaotic streets ebb and flow with a heady brew of old and new.

Jaipur is part of the Golden Triangle tourist circuit along with Delhi and Agra. It hosts several attractions like the City Palace, Govind Dev Ji Temple, Vidhan Sabha, Birla Temple, several massive Rajput forts, and so much more.

Highlights

Amber Fort
Hawa Mahal
City Palace
Jantar Mantar
Local Bazaars
Rajasthani Cuisine

Best Time to Visit

October to March is the best time to visit Jaipur when the weather is pleasant and perfect for sightseeing. Avoid summer months (April to June) when temperatures can soar above 40°C.

Beaches of Goa

Goa is a state on the southwestern coast of India within the region known as the Konkan. It is bounded by the state of Maharashtra to the north and Karnataka to the east and south, with the Arabian Sea forming its western coast.

Goa is India's smallest state by area and the fourth-smallest by population. It has the highest GDP per capita among all Indian states, two and a half times that of the country. Panaji is the state's capital, while Vasco da Gama is its largest city. The historic city of Margao still exhibits the cultural influence of the Portuguese, who first landed in the early 16th century.

Highlights

Calangute Beach
Baga Beach
Anjuna Beach
Water Sports
Nightlife
Portuguese Architecture

Best Time to Visit

November to February is the best time to visit Goa when the weather is pleasant and perfect for beach activities. The monsoon season (June to September) offers a different experience with lush green landscapes.

Kerala Backwaters

The Kerala backwaters are a network of interconnected canals, rivers, lakes, and inlets, a labyrinthine system formed by more than 900 km of waterways. In the midst of this landscape, there are a number of towns and cities, which serve as the starting and end points of backwater cruises.

The backwaters were formed by the action of waves and shore currents creating low barrier islands across the mouths of the many rivers flowing down from the Western Ghats range. The Kerala Backwaters are a chain of brackish lagoons and lakes lying parallel to the Arabian Sea coast.

Highlights

Houseboat Stays
Alleppey
Kumarakom
Local Village Life
Ayurvedic Treatments
Kerala Cuisine

Best Time to Visit

September to March is the best time to visit Kerala Backwaters when the weather is pleasant. The monsoon season (June to August) offers a unique experience with lush green landscapes but may limit some activities.

Holy Ghats of Varanasi

Varanasi, also known as Benares or Kashi, is a city on the banks of the river Ganges in Uttar Pradesh, India. A major religious hub in India, it is the holiest of the seven sacred cities in Hinduism and Jainism, and played an important role in the development of Buddhism.

The city is known worldwide for its many ghats, embankments made in steps of stone slabs along the river bank where pilgrims perform ritual ablutions. The Dashashwamedh Ghat is the main and probably the oldest ghat of Varanasi located on the Ganges, close to the Kashi Vishwanath Temple.

Highlights

Dashashwamedh Ghat
Manikarnika Ghat
Kashi Vishwanath Temple
Evening Aarti
Boat Rides
Sarnath

Best Time to Visit

October to March is the best time to visit Varanasi when the weather is pleasant. Dev Deepawali in November and Maha Shivaratri in February/March are special times to experience the city's spiritual energy.

Tea Gardens of Darjeeling

Darjeeling is a town in India's West Bengal state, in the Himalayan foothills. Once a summer resort for the British Raj elite, it remains the terminus of the narrow-gauge Darjeeling Himalayan Railway, or "Toy Train," completed in 1881.

It's famed for the distinctive black tea grown on plantations that dot its surrounding slopes. Its backdrop is Mt. Kanchenjunga, among the world's highest peaks. Darjeeling is internationally renowned for its tea industry and the Darjeeling Himalayan Railway, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Highlights

Tea Plantations
Tiger Hill
Toy Train Ride
Himalayan Views
Batasia Loop
Peace Pagoda

Best Time to Visit

March to May and September to November are the best times to visit Darjeeling when the weather is pleasant and offers clear views of the Himalayas. Avoid the monsoon season (June to August) when landslides are common.
